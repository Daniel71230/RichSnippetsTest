Welcome to Joe's Grill

Joe's Grill, a culinary landmark with a storied past, offers a dining experience steeped in tradition. Since its transformation from the Johnston family residence in 1966, Joe's Grill has become a beloved institution, known for its commitment to quality ingredients and local sourcing. Celebrate your special day with us and enjoy a complimentary birthday meal, dine in style in our mid-century modern 'burger stand', and savor the flavors of our American and Barbecue cuisine, crafted from the freshest produce of The Farm at Agritopia.

Discover our American and Barbecue dishes at Joe's Grill, located at 123 Elm St, Sometown, CA.
